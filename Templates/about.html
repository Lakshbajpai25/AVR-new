Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?

Our tool supports popular video formats such as MP4, MOV, AVI, and more.

Does the tool work on mobile devices?

Yes! The tool is accessible via web browsers on both desktop and mobile devices and also can be downloaded in desktop.

Can I manually adjust the framing?

Absolutely! While the AI handles most of the work, you can make manual tweaks if needed.

How long does processing take?

Processing times depend on video length and resolution, but most videos are reframed within minutes.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all uploaded files are encrypted, and we do not store your videos after processing.

Can the tool handle high-resolution videos?

Yes, our tool supports up to 4K resolution, ensuring high-quality reframing without loss of detail.
